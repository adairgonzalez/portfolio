## Overview

EduCreate is a full-stack AI education platform that transforms the way teachers plan lessons and students prepare study materials. Built with React, TypeScript, and modern web technologies, it delivers significant time savings and efficiency improvements in educational content creation.

## Key Features

- **Intelligent Content Engine**: Processes 5 different file formats with 98% accuracy, generating classroom materials in under 60 seconds
- **Customized Study Guides**: Produces personalized study materials in 6 different time-based formats, ranging from 5-minute reviews to 2-week comprehensive plans
- **Time-Saving Solution**: Reduces lesson planning time by 70% for teachers and cuts study material preparation time from 3 hours to just 30 minutes
- **AI-Powered Backend**: Leverages advanced AI models integrated with Supabase for intelligent content processing and generation

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Supabase, AI/ML Models
- **Infrastructure**: Cloud-based architecture for scalable processing
